THE CLOISTER

- This is a html version of The Cloister
- It is suggested that you have a copy of the pdf to hand (can be found here)
- In order to play the game, choose between the two of you which will be the Pilot, and which will be the Operator
- Then click the appropriate button, following the prompts as you go
- While progressing, make sure that both of you have no more instructions to complete before pressing next
- The prompts will tell you to ask questions, once the question is asked feel free to press next.
- A prompt may ask you to give information to the other player, the details for this may not be given, feel free to make up details or be vauge about what is being told to the other player
- If there apears to be a problem, reloading the page will reset the progress and should fix things
